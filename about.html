Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a seamless, secure, and user-friendly platform for vaccination management. We aim to empower communities by offering easy booking, campaign reviews, and reliable medical information.

What is this system?

This is a vaccination management platform designed to simplify dose booking and ensure organized vaccine campaigns.

Who can use this system?

Patients and doctors can register and use the system to book doses, manage profiles, and oversee campaigns.

This system facilitates vaccination campaigns, allowing patients to book doses, view schedules, and leave reviews. Doctors can manage campaigns and schedules.

Patients can register using their NID, book a dose by selecting an available date, and receive automated scheduling for the next dose. Doctors add and manage vaccine campaigns.

We prioritize your privacy. All personal information, including NID, is stored securely and used only for vaccination purposes.

- Register as a patient or doctor.
- Log in to access the dashboard.
- Patients can view available campaigns and book doses.
- Doctors can add, edit, or delete campaigns and schedules.
